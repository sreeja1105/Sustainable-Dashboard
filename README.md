# Sustainable Fashion Dashboard (Power BI)

A Power BI dashboard analyzing sustainability trends in the global fashion industry using the **Sustainable Fashion Trends 2024** dataset.  
This project explores how brands, materials, and countries contribute to environmental impact — helping stakeholders make informed, eco‑conscious decisions.

---

##  Project Overview

The fashion industry is undergoing a major shift toward sustainability.  
This dashboard provides insights into:

- Carbon footprint by material and country  
- Water usage and environmental impact  
- Brand presence and sustainability ratings  
- Eco‑friendly manufacturing practices  
- Year‑over‑year sustainability trends  

The goal is to support data‑driven decision‑making for a greener fashion future.

---

##  Key Insights

- **Material Impact:** Hemp and Bamboo show lower carbon emissions compared to traditional materials.  
- **Country Comparison:** Italy, China, Germany, and Brazil lead in brand presence and environmental footprint.  
- **Eco‑Friendly Manufacturing:** Countries vary significantly in sustainable production adoption.  
- **Trend Analysis:** Sustainability ratings show fluctuating progress over the decade.
